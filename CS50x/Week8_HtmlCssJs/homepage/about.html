About Coffee Haven

Established in 2018 by founder and head barista Lena Moore, Coffee Haven began as a cozy dream tucked into a quiet corner of the city. Inspired by Lena’s travels through Europe’s café culture, the space was designed to be a warm, welcoming retreat from the everyday rush. With a focus on ethically sourced beans, handcrafted pastries, and community connection, Coffee Haven has grown from a small neighborhood spot into a beloved local hangout. Whether you're here for your morning espresso or an afternoon with friends, Coffee Haven is your home away from home, where every cup tells a story.
