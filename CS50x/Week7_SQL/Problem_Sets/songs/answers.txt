1. If songs.db contains the top 100 songs of one listener from 2018, how would you characterize their audio aura?
- Spotify uses the average values of energy, valence and danceability to determine a user's audio aura.
- We do not need "LIMIT 100" at the end of the sql statement since songs.db only contains the top 100 songs.

SELECT AVG(energy) AS avg_energy,
    AVG(valence) AS avg_valence,
    AVG(danceability) AS avg_danceability
    FROM songs;
