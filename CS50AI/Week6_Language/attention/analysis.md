# Analysis

## Layer 7, Head 12

This attention head pays the most attention to word before it. It follows a pattern from top left to bottom right.

Example Sentences:
- The expensive [MASK] was stolen.
- Mary is [MASK] her fried noodles.

## Layer 12, Head 4

This attention head pays the most attention to punctuations that divide the logic of the sentence, as well as the [SEP] token.

Example Sentences:
- The [MASK] barked loudly in the park.
- Hurry up, or the [MASK] will be late!
